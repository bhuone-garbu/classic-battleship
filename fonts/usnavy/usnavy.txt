US Navy Truetype Font for Windows (version 1)

2007 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

http://www.iconian.com

This is a dingbat font with over 90 glyph symbols of US naval ships. This font comes in 4 versions - Regular, Regular Mirrored, 3D and 3D Mirrored.

Each ship symbol has a mirrored counterpart between the upper and lower cases. Use of a character map program, such as Windows' Character Map, will be very helpful in exploring and using this font.
