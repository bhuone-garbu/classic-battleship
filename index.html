Game rules  

Players

- 2

Goal

- Sink all your opponents ships

Setup

- Place your fleets on the bottom side of the board

- The number next to fleet name represents the size of cells

Rules

- Players starts first

- Each player takes turn and whoever destroys all the fleets first, wins. Simple!
